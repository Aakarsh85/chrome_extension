Smart Bookmark Help

Saving a Bookmark

Click the extension icon and press "Save Current Page".

Searching Bookmarks

Use the search bar in the popup to quickly find saved bookmarks.

Deleting Bookmarks

Click the ✕ button next to any bookmark to remove it.

Keyboard Shortcut

You can press Ctrl + Shift + S to save the current page instantly.
